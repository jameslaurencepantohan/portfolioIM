Cebu-Bohol Educational Tour 2025

My journey through technology companies and historical sites in Cebu and Bohol.

Tour Timeline & Experiences

November 19, 2025

Day 1 - WorldTech & Historical Site Visit

Visited one of the Trusted and Famous technology companies in Cebu including WorldTech Information Solutions Inc. We learn about how Cybersecurity works and how to deal with hackers and mailwares. They Teach how real-world of IT works and gives us advice and insights of what path we take in the world of Tech. Lastly we visited one of the famous tourist spot in cebu called Lapu-Lapu.

Technology Career Insights Networking

November 20, 2025

Day 2 - Rivan IT Cebu and Codechum

In the second day we visited the Rivan IT Cebu, This Company is a training center. They are specialize in IT and Networking certifications courses like Cisco and Linux. During our visits they teach us the basics of networking and how they setups, configure the network like call center company and they give also insights and idea what are the possible path we could possibly take after we graduate.Before the day ends we also visited Codechum and talk to the Co-Founder of CodeChum We learn many things and enjoy during our visit.

History Career Insights Technology

November 21, 2025

Day 3 - MATA Technologies Inc.

In the 3rd day we visit the MATA Technologies Inc. and this company is a homegrown provider of Virtual tours for real estate in the Philippines. During our visit they introduce to us how they create the virtual tours project and other project that they already made like virtual games. They give also ideas on creating our own game application, give us advice and inspirations to continue our journey in the IT industry in the future. After that we go to cebu city hall to deliver our donations for the affected of the calamity in Cebu.

Virtual Reality Game Development Public Service

November 22, 2025

Day 4 - Visited the CDRRMO of Tagbilaran and Sekatunas Mirror of the World

In the last day we visited the CDRRMO of tagbilaran and they show us how they work and how hard to handle a single city and they shares pre cautions and tips that will help us whenerver we are in danger. After that we go in Sekatunas Mirror of the World and takes picture buy souvenirs the place is so amazing and enjoyable.
